How parts link to bookings

auto-reserved on booking

When a service is auto-booked, Pulse instantly reserves the right components per raft (CO₂ cylinders, hydrostatic release units, flare sets, ration packs) plus per-booking consumables. Reserved stock is committed; completing a service consumes it; cancelling releases it back.
